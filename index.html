Fly Tweety Fly

Are you ready to spread your wings and soar through the skies? Embark on an endless adventure in Fly Tweety Fly, the exhilarating endless runner game where you control a colorful bird on a mission to collect feathers and new tweety's.

Tap to make your bird jump and fly and navigate through a series of obstacles. Collect feathers along the way to earn points and unlock new characters. Watch out for challenging obstacles and tricky traps that stand in your way!

  • Simple one-touch controls: Tap to flap your wings fly!
  • Endless fun: Keep flying and see how far you can go!
  • Unlockable characters from egg suprises: Collect feathers to unlock new and colorful birds!

Challenge your friends: Compete for the highest score and prove you're the ultimate flyer!
